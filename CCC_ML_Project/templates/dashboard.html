// SECTION 05 — NOTABLE OBSERVATIONS

Field Observations

Supplementary intelligence notes drawn from the cluster boundaries and inter-cluster comparisons.

// OBS-01 · VICTIM AGE

Demographic Skew Is Cluster-Specific

Average victim age splits meaningfully across clusters. High-crime metros skew younger; low-crime cities show older victim profiles — suggesting distinct social exposure patterns per archetype.

// OBS-02 · GEOGRAPHY

Geography Does Not Predict Cluster Membership

Cities in the same state, or even the same region, often land in different clusters. Behavioral similarity is driven by socioeconomic and enforcement characteristics — not physical proximity.

// OBS-03 · CLOSURE RATE

Case Closure Rate Is The Weakest Signal

Despite being included as a feature, case closure rate had the lowest discriminating power in the PCA projection. Its variance across all clusters was relatively uniform — suggesting systemic consistency regardless of crime level.
